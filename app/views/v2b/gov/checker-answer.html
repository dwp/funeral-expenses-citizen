What you need to apply for a Funeral Expenses Payment

{#

You'll need:

#}

Your details

  • your National Insurance number
  • your address and contact details
  • your bank or building society details

About the person who died

    {% if (data['relationship-radio'] == "partner") %}
  • your partner’s National Insurance number
  • the date your partner died
    • {% endif %} {% if (data['relationship-radio'] == "child") %}
  • your parent's National Insurance number
  • the date your parent died
    • {% endif %} {% if (data['relationship-radio'] == "parent") %}
  • your child's National Insurance number (if they have one)
  • the date your child died
    • {% endif %} {% if (data['relationship-radio'] == "relative") %}
  • your relative or friend's National Insurance number
  • the date your relative or friend died
    • {% endif %} {% if (data['relationship-radio'] == "child" or data['relationship-radio'] == "parent") %}
  • details of any other surviving children or parents of the person who died
    • {% endif %}

Funeral details

    {% if (data['arranging-radio'] == "funeral-director") %}
  • name and address of funeral director
    • {% endif %} {% if (data['arranging-radio'] == "organisation") %}
  • name and address of the organisation or person from the community arranging the funeral
    • {% endif %} {% if (data['arranging-radio'] == "myself") %}
  • date of the funeral
    • {% endif %}

About the estate

{% if (data['finance-radio'] == "responsible") %}

We'll ask you for details about the estate of the person who died, such as:

  • who is taking responsibility for their financial affairs
  • what money that had
  • any life insurance policies, pensions or funeral plans
{% endif %} {% if (data['finance-radio'] == "not-responsible") %}
  • contact details of the person dealing with the financial affairs of the person who died
{% endif %}
