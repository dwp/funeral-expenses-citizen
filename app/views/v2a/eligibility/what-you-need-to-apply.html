Apply for Funeral Expenses Payment

It takes about 25 minutes to apply. You cannot save your application and come back later.

Do you have everything you need to apply?

You'll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your partner’s National Insurance number
  • the date your partner died
  • details of the funeral and funeral director, if you’re using one
  • your address and contact details
  • your bank or building society details

If you're responsible for the financial affairs of the person who died, you'll also need details about their estate.
