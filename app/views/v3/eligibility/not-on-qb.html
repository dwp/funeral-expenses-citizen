You or your partner must get a qualifying benefit to get a Funeral Expenses Payment

{% else %}

You must be getting a benefit to apply for a Funeral Expenses Payment

{% endif %}

You cannot make a claim for Funeral Expenses Payment if you’re not getting, or waiting to hear about, one of the following:

  • Universal Credit
  • Income Support
  • Income-based Jobseeker’s Allowance
  • Income-related Employment and Support Allowance
  • Pension Credit
  • Housing Benefit
  • Support for Mortgage Interest

If you're eligible for a benefit but have not applied yet

You'll need to make your claim after you apply for a benefit and within 6 months of the funeral.

You can find out what benefits you may be entitled to by going to GOV.UK and searching for 'benefits calculator'.
